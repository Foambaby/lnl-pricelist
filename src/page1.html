Sound Services
Select both an Input option and an Output option to best fit your event type and size. Then, choose any Add-ons for more speakers or wireless microhpones.
Inputs
Every sound event has inputs. Inputs include microphones, instruments, laptops, phones, etc.

  • Large

Add-ons
  • Wireless Microphone
    $10/ea
    Wireless microphones can easily be added to any input level, increasing flexibility for the performer and reducing stage clutter.
  • Subwoofers
    $50
    Subwoofers add more bass. Great for concerts, bands, or just playing music.
  • Stage Monitors
    $30
    Speakers for people on the stage. Highly recommended for bands and on-stage performances.
  • Area Fills
    $20
    Additional speakers to fill a large or oddly-shaped venue.
How to Book an Event
  1. Submit a work order using our online tool at lnl.wpi.edu/workorder more than 2 weeks ahead of your event.
  2. Contact us if you would like to discuss your event in person.
  3. Your work order will be reviewed, and we will contact you with any questions.
  4. Your event will be approved once LNL determines whether services can be provided.
