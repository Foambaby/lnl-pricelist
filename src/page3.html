Other Services
Rigging
Rigging involves hanging truss from the ceiling to allow for overhead mounting of lights. The workorder submission tool will help you determine if rigging is recommended.
  • Basic
    $60
    A single length of truss. Often necessary for events in Alden, Harrington, Odeum, Recreation Center Courts, and the Quad.
  • Advanced
    $120
    Multiple lengths of truss. Most often used for events in Alden and Harrington.
Power Distribution
$40
Heavy duty power distribution for your large equipment such as food warmers or power to many tables for attendees. Not necessary for small electronics that just need an extension cord.
Projection
LNL offers 16mm, 35mm, and 70mm film as well as digital projection (DP) services in the Fuller Upper theater. For more details and full projection pricing, please see lnl.wpi.edu/pricelist-full.
